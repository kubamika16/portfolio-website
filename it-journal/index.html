Designing an Entry Structure for Individual Journals

9 May 2023

Introduction ðŸŒŸ

  I came up with the idea of sharing my journals in an easy-to-read and simple format. Each journal entry will have a main tile containing smaller sections (usually 4) with the following titles: Introduction, Tasks Completed, Challenges Faced, and Resources. The main journal tile will display the title and the posting date.

Tasks Completed ðŸŽ¯

  • Expanded the existing it-journal structure by adding new classes and applying specific styles

Challenges Faced ðŸš¨

  • Challenge 1: I worked on the existing structure of the general "it-journal" page, as I wanted a similar layout. I faced a problem when trying to remove default properties for the journal entry title. I discovered that I could resolve this issue using specific events for "pointer-events" and "cursor."
  • Challenge 2: I wanted to create a gap between the four small tiles (Introduction, Tasks, Challenges, Resources) while ensuring they were evenly distributed across the parent div. The "flex-grow: 1" property helped me achieve this.
